About Thalapathy Vijay

Joseph Vijay Chandrasekhar, known professionally as Thalapathy Vijay, is an Indian actor, playback singer, and philanthropist who works predominantly in Tamil cinema. He was born on June 22, 1974, in Chennai, Tamil Nadu, India.

Vijay made his acting debut as a lead actor with the film "Naalaiya Theerpu" in 1992. Since then, he has appeared in numerous successful films and has established himself as one of the leading actors in the Tamil film industry.

Known for his versatility and charisma, Vijay has received several awards and nominations throughout his career, including multiple Tamil Nadu State Film Awards and Filmfare Awards South.

Off-screen, Vijay is involved in various philanthropic activities and has contributed to several charitable organizations. He is also known for his strong fan following and is often referred to as "Thalapathy" by his fans.
